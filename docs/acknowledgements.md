This project exists because of the public LLM Wiki idea space and the people who documented, implemented, and explained it.

## 💡 Idea and Prior Art

- **Andrej Karpathy**: For publishing the [LLM Wiki idea file](https://gist.github.com/karpathy/442a6bf555914893e9891c11519de94f), which framed the core pattern of using an LLM agent to maintain a persistent Markdown knowledge base.
- **Antigravity**: For the article [Karpathy's LLM Wiki: The Complete Guide to His Idea File](https://antigravity.codes/blog/karpathy-llm-wiki-idea-file), which helped explain the idea file and related tooling.
- **Tahir Balarabe**: For [What is LLM Wiki Pattern? Persistent Knowledge with LLM Wikis](https://medium.com/@tahirbalarabe2/what-is-llm-wiki-pattern-persistent-knowledge-with-llm-wikis-3227f561abc1), which clearly describes the shift from retrieval to accumulated knowledge.
- **rohitg00**: For [LLM Wiki v2](https://gist.github.com/rohitg00/2067ab416f7bbe447c1977edaaa681e2), especially the discussion of lifecycle, confidence, stale knowledge, and operational lessons.
- **Pratiyush** & **SamurAIGPT**: Thanks to the authors of [Pratiyush/llm-wiki](https://github.com/Pratiyush/llm-wiki) and [SamurAIGPT/llm-wiki-agent](https://github.com/SamurAIGPT/llm-wiki-agent/tree/main) for public implementations and workflow examples around agent-maintained knowledge bases.
